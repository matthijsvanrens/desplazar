• «Pestrechinka» Ltd

      «Pestrechinka» Ltd is a specialized trading enterprise in the structure of «Ak Bars» Holding company.  The enterprise was established in 2009 on the basis of Trade house «Pestrechinka» founded in 2004.
      «Pestrechinka» Ltd sales poultry meat products produced by Poultry farming unit of «Ak Bars» Holding company OJSC  («Poultry plant «Kazanskaya» OJSC, «Agrofirm «Ak Bars-Pestretsy» OJSC, «Poultry farming complex «Ak Bars» Ltd) and is considered to be an important part of vertically integrated agroindustrial chain of «Ak Bars» Holding.
      The enterprise carries out wholesale, small-wholesale and retail delivery of products under the trade mark «Pestrechinka» for internal and regional companies, warehouses and storehouses, commercial networks and distribution centers, supermarkets and shops. Producers make direct control over delivery of certified tested products that enables to guarantee high quality and ecological safety of poultry meat products to customers. Total area of «Pestrechinka» Ltd warehouses is more than 4 thousand square meters, equipped with refrigerating chambers with temperature from 0° up to -22°C, as well as “shock” freeze chamber (-33°C).
      Owing to the network of authorized distributors poultry plants products are delivered to many regions of Russia: Moscow and Moscow region, Ural, Siberia and Volga region. Range of products sold by the company counts more than one hundred items: raw poultry meat and semi-products, wide range of sausage goods, smoked and semi-smoked products. Products of poultry plants are regularly rewarded with golden and silver medals for high quality and superior taste and are in great demand with «Pestrechinka» Ltd partners.
      Specialists of “Pestrechinka” Ltd have professional knowledge and great experience in production and sale of meat products. With due regard to requests of regular partners the company provides discount system and commodity credit. Managers of the company guarantee accurate and operative work of warehouse and are always ready to provide complete package of accompanying and certification documents.
      Taking into account the growing demand for poultry meat and semi-finished products along with the growth of finished meat products consumption, in 2009 «Ak Bars» Holding company realized the project on construction of poultry meat processing plant. The new plant production capacity is up to 13 thousand tons of finished products per year. Additional product assortment comprises about one hundred names of poultry meat products: sausage goods, smoked products, meat rolls, ham etc. New products are sold under the trade mark “Pestrechinka” which is well-known to regional customers. Taking into account the increase of product line and the growth of production volume, expansion of distribution on Russian market and development of trade mark “Pestrechinka” up to federal level brand have become the main strategic goals of “Pestrechinka” Ltd.
