      REPUBLIC OF TATARSTAN

      From «Bugulma Aviation Enterprise» to «AK BARS AERO»

      In 2005 «Bugulma Aviation Enterprise» joined the «Holding Company «Ak Bars» which gave a new impulse to the
      development of the airline.

      On the 29th of January 2010 one of the best transportation enterprises in the Republic of Tatarstan got a new
      name – «AK BARS AERO».

      Aircraft Stock

      Within 18 months AK BARS AERO bought and has been operating 7 regional passenger aircraft CRJ-200LR by the
      Canadian corporation Bombardier.

      By the end of 2012 the CRJ-200 stock is planned to be expanded to 15 units.

      The airline has an up-to-date technical base for maintenance of CRJ-200 and trained qualified personnel. The line
      maintenance bases of the airline are located at the airports of Bugulma and Kazan.

    • UNIVERSIADE KAZAN 2013

      The mission of the Kazan Universiade is to gather young athletes from all over the world in the city of unique multiethnic heritage.

      To continue Russian sport traditions, become a launch pad for future generations of athletes.

      To make the life of Kazan citizens more interesting and convenient.

      Give our children a possibility to do any kind of sport they like, to lead a healthy way of life.

      To make a significant contribution into the creation of Russia’s positive image in the world.

    • UNIVERSIADE KAZAN 2013

      In July 6 – 17, 2013 the capital of the Republic of Tatarstan, Kazan city, renowned for its hospitality, will become the centre of the key international multi-sport event – the XXVII World Summer Universiade.

      More than 13500 athletes and official delegation members from 170 countries of the world as well as more than 1500 media representatives are expected to arrive in Kazan.

      About 100 thousand tourists and fans from Russia and abroad will visit Kazan both to support their countries and get to know a millennial historical heritage of the city, its rich culture and modern infrastructure.

      Uni – the winged snow leopard kitten has become the mascot of the Universiade 2013. Winged snow leopard is a national symbol of Tatarstan and it appears on the emblem of the Republic.

      The brand sign of the XXVII World University Games inspired by a tulip – an essential element of a traditional Tatar ornament – is a symbol of spring, youth and development, joy and happiness.
