• ABOUT THE
      REPUBLIC OF TATARSTAN

      From «Bugulma Aviation Enterprise» to «AK BARS AERO»

      In 2005 «Bugulma Aviation Enterprise» joined the «Holding Company «Ak Bars» which gave a new impulse to the
      development of the airline.

      On the 29th of January 2010 one of the best transportation enterprises in the Republic of Tatarstan got a new
      name – «AK BARS AERO».

      Aircraft Stock

      Within 18 months AK BARS AERO bought and has been operating 7 regional passenger aircraft CRJ-200LR by the
      Canadian corporation Bombardier.

      By the end of 2012 the CRJ-200 stock is planned to be expanded to 15 units.

      The airline has an up-to-date technical base for maintenance of CRJ-200 and trained qualified personnel. The line
      maintenance bases of the airline are located at the airports of Bugulma and Kazan.
