      INFORMATION

      “Kazan Helicopters” Joint-Stock Company is a member of the Russian helicopter construction holding “Russian Helicopters”.

      “Kazan Helicopters” Joint-Stock Company is well known worldwide as the manufacturer of the Mil Mi-8/17 family of helicopters.

      “Kazan Helicopters” have experience in aircraft construction since the 1940s, serial production since 1951 and in the international market since 1956. High reliability, ruggedness, safety, and ease in control and maintenance are the characteristics of the aircraft produced by “Kazan Helicopters”.

      Within the whole history of “Kazan Helicopters” more than 12,000 of Mi-4, Mi-8, Mi-14, Mi-17, ANSAT helicopters and their modifications were delivered to 100 countries of the world.

      “Kazan Helicopters” is approved by the Aviation Register of the Russian Federation as a design centre for civil helicopters and certified for training and advanced training of ground and flight personnel.

      KAZAN HELICOPTERS

      “Kazan Helicopters” Joint-Stock Company is a member of the Russian helicopter construction holding “Russian Helicopters”.

      “Kazan Helicopters” Joint-Stock Company is well known worldwide as the manufacturer of the Mil Mi-8/17 family of helicopters.

      “Kazan Helicopters” have experience in aircraft construction since the 1940s, serial production since 1951 and in the international market since 1956. High reliability, ruggedness, safety, and ease in control and maintenance are the characteristics of the aircraft produced by “Kazan Helicopters”.

      Within the whole history of “Kazan Helicopters” more than 12,000 of Mi-4, Mi-8, Mi-14, Mi-17, ANSAT helicopters and their modifications were delivered to 100 countries of the world.

      The brand sign of the XXVII World University Games inspired by a tulip – an essential element of a traditional Tatar ornament – is a symbol of spring, youth and development, joy and happiness.

      The Latin letter “U” is clearly recognized in the brand sign as well as five multi-coloured stars which symbolize the five continents.

      “Kazan Helicopters” is approved by the Aviation Register of the Russian Federation as a design centre for civil helicopters and certified for training and advanced training of ground and flight personnel.

      “Kazan Helicopters” executes the whole cycle of helicopter’s construction from development and serial production till post-sale support, personnel training and carrying out repair. System of production quality certified in accordance with ISO 9001 standards, guarantees the due control of the produced and repaired helicopters.

      Helicopters produced in Kazan can perform following missions:

      Utility transport; Passenger and VIP transport; Search and rescue; medical evacuation; military operations; training.

      Kazan Helicopter Plant renders services which include the whole life cycle of the delivered helicopters:

      supply of spare parts on AOG terms; scheduled supply of spare parts; overhaul and repair of helicopters; technical maintenance of helicopters (periodic operations); execution of all types of modernization and upgrade programs; supply of ground and mission equipment; engineering and counseling services; supply of technical documentation; training of flight and ground personnel in Aviation training center.
