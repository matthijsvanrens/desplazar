• ABOUT COMPANY

      Since more than 60 years “Kazancompressormash” OJSC has been specializing in the development of centrifugal and screw compressors used for compression of actually all industrial gases. Compressor plants are delivered to more than 60 countries of the world.

      Horizontally split centrifugal compressor plants for capacity up to 1400cu.m/min and end pressure up to 4.5 MPa (45 kgf/cm2);
      Vertically split centrifugal compressor plants for capacity up to 500cu.m/min and end pressure up to 45 MPa (450 kgf/cm2);
      Multi-shaft geared centrifugal compressors for capacity up to 1000cu.m/min and end pressure up to 5 MPa (50 kgf/cm2);
      Air turbo-refrigerating machines for cooling capacity up to 60 kW;
      Expander-compressor turbo-units up to 2.5 MW;
      Gas piping units for pressure up to 10 MPa (100 kgf/cm2) driven by gas turbine engine up to 25 MW;
      Centrifugal and screw compressor based refrigerating machines and units for various refrigerants and for cooling capacity up to 12 MW;
      Automobile chassis mounted Oxygen and Nitrogen gas charging stations for end pressure up to 35 MPa (350 kgf/cm2);
      Oil free screw compressors for capacity up to 200 cu.m/min and end pressure up to 2 MPa (20 kgf/cm2);
      Oil injected free screw compressors for capacity up to 100 cu.m/min and end pressure up to 5 MPa (50 kgf/cm2);
