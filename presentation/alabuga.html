      INFORMATION

      The territory of the SEZ “Alabuga” is 20 km². The master plan is based on the best examples of the international
      experience. Each plot has access to all necessary utilities — gas, electricity, water, heat, sewerage, telecommunication
      lines. There is no connection fee for SEZ “Alabuga” utilities. The Government has already invested up to USD 800 mln.

      The Free Customs Zone

      It is advantageous to place modern, hi-tech production that requires importation of equipment and components in the
      SEZ “Alabuga”. Equipment and goods are exempt from import duties and VAT for the duration of their presence on the
      territory of the zone. In addition, there is no export duty when the equipment leaves the zone. There is a state-of-the-art
      sixty hectare customs terminal specifically for the use of zone residents. Cargo can be transferred to the customs
      terminal via main roads and the railway system. The railway network of the zone is connected to the Russian Federal
      Railway system, giving investors the opportunity to ship cargo to the Black Sea, the Baltic Sea or the Pacific Ocean.
      There is a special container site for keeping up to 1,500 pieces of railroad cargoes and processing 100 pieces of cargo
      per day. The network of modern roads within the zone provides the opportunity to transfer cargo directly from the
      entrance to the residents’ area.

      HOW TO BECOME A RESIDENT

      To conduct an industrial-production activity in SEZ «Alabuga» using all the tax and customs incentives, a company must get
      a resident status. A resident of an industrial special economic zone is recognized as a business entity, except from a unitary
      enterprise, registered on the territory of the municipality within which the special economic zone is located and who signed
      an agreement on industrial-production or logistics activity with the managing company. Residents of industrial special
      economic zone may carry out only industrial production activity within the limits stipulated by the agreement on
      industrial-production activity.
