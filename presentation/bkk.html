      INFORMATION

      Welcome to the Bread-baking and Confectionery Plant, OJSC, founded in 1986 in Kazan. For 25 years it has been an
      industry leader in the Republic of Tatarstan, Russia.

      The plant is equipped with modern automated production lines from leading European vendors. A highly efficient climate
      control system is employed to ensure superior quality of our bread 365 days a year.

      Our company participates in all major industrial contests and food shows of regional and federal status. In late 2011, we
      were proud to be awarded the “Taste of Quality” and “Top 100 Russian products” prizes that attest to our nationwide
      recognition.

      Since 2004, the Bread-baking and Confectionery Plant of Kazan has been annually certified for compliance with the
      requirements of the international standards in management and quality, ISO 9001 and ISO 22000.

      Another standard that is strategically important for us to meet is the Halal Production requirements. Since 2005,
      when we started certifying our products for the Muslim market, the number of Halal items we produce has reached
      70 and counting. The compliance with Halal requirements is guaranteed by the Committee on the standard “Halal” in
      the Spiritual Administration of Muslims of Tatarstan. We are convinced that Halal is a very significant quality mark for
      millions of our customers, and we proudly introduce several new Halal-certified items every year.
