  • Holding company OJSC

    INFORMATION

    Ak Bars Holding company is a vertically integrated diversified republican holding company. It consolidates more than
    85 industrial, construction, agricultural, food, trade and transport enterprises of the Republic of Tatarstan.

    Employees – more than 38 thousand persons

    Annual turnover – more than 2,4 billion US dollars

    Company vision

    Ak Bars Holding company OJSC is one of the best management companies incorporating high-efficient and competitive
    enterprises of different branches of activity.

    Company mission

    Ak Bars Holding company OJSC incorporates companies with a purpose of their further growth for the benefit of the
    Republic of Tatarstan economy development.

  • Strategic goals

    INFORMATION

    Formation of the leading agroindustrial complex providing food supply safety of the Republic of Tatarstan.

    Development of the complex of financially stable industrial enterprises, selling competitive products on national
    and international markets.

    Leadership on construction and air transportation markets of the Republic of Tatarstan, strengthening positions at
    the federal level.

    Formation of retail trade network of the federal level.

    Optimization of the Holding company investment portfolio, expansion of presence in new high-technology
    branches of activity.
